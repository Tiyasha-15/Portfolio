About Portfolio

Converting Handwrtting Into Text

In this project, we compiled a dataset consisting of handwritten letters, leveraging the dataset to recognize various letter patterns. Utilizing Matplotlib, we visually represented these patterns through plots. Despite initially employing the K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) algorithm, the achieved accuracy did not meet our expectations. As a solution, we developed a Sequential Neural Network, which significantly improved accuracy rates, showcasing the effectiveness of this approach in letter recognition tasks.
